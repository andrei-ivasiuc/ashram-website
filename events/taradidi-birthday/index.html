Tara didi's birthday

Our beloved and legendary mentor, Tara Didi turned 85 on 5th July. Staying true to her selfless nature and showing her love for Mother Earth, she celebrated her birthday by planting saplings . This day resonated with the energetic spirit of Tara Didi and was packed with many events like the live streaming of cultural program held at Kechla, Odisha, distribution of snacks among workers, inauguration of an exhibition of spiritual paintings by Usha Patel, musical offering by Ashram inmates and Tara Didiâ€™s reading at Meditation hall. Many well-wishers who arrived to greet her during the day gave her plants as a token of love they had for this living legend.

The day came to an end with meditation session, post which Taradi read out from Four-Aspect of The Mother.
